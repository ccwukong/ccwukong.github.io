Privacy Policy for KopiBot Kit

Last Updated: September 28, 2025

Welcome to KopiBot Kit. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit our website, kopibotkit.com.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data from you when you voluntarily provide it:

  • Email Address: When you sign up for our early access waiting list or contact us.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personal information about how you interact with our website, such as your browser type and pages visited, to help us improve our service.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To add you to our early access waiting list and to notify you when KopiBot Kit launches.
  • To send you information about our product, including special offers like the founder's discount.
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests.

3. Data Protection and Storage

We are committed to protecting your data. We use reputable third-party services to store and manage your information and take reasonable technical measures to prevent unauthorized access. All data is processed and stored in secure environments.

4. Your Rights under the PDPA

As a user in Singapore, you have rights over your personal data under the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA). You have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for us to use your data. You can do this at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or by contacting us directly.

5. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. If you sign up for our waiting list, we will keep your email address until the product has launched and our initial notification campaign is complete, or until you choose to unsubscribe.

6. Third-Party Disclosure

We will not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to outside parties.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at nick.chen@kopibotkit.com.
